🌲 Discover Rochester, Washington

🏞️ Parks & Outdoor Spaces

  • Swede Hall Park: A peaceful green space near the historic Swede Hall, perfect for community gatherings and outdoor relaxation.
  • Black River Habitat Management Area: A nature preserve ideal for hiking, birdwatching, and spotting local wildlife along the Black River.
  • Millersylvania State Park: Just minutes away, this popular state park offers camping, swimming, hiking trails, and beautiful forested picnic areas.

🏫 Schools

Rochester is served by the Rochester School District, known for its supportive community and strong educational programs:

  • Rochester Primary School: Grades K–2
  • Grand Mound Elementary: Grades 3–5
  • Rochester Middle School: Grades 6–8
  • Rochester High School: Grades 9–12

🎯 Local Attractions

  • Lucky Eagle Casino & Hotel: Located nearby, it offers gaming, events, restaurants, and a comfortable hotel stay for guests and visitors.
  • Great Wolf Lodge (Grand Mound): A family favorite, this indoor waterpark resort is just minutes from Rochester and features fun for all ages.

Rochester blends rural charm with accessibility to amenities in nearby towns. It's a great spot for families, outdoor lovers, and anyone who enjoys the balance of small-town living and convenience.
