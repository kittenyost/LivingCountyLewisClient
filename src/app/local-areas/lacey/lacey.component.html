🌲 Discover Lacey, Washington

🏞️ Parks

  • Woodland Creek Community Park: A large park with trails, a fishing lake, picnic shelters, and the Lacey Community Center.
  • Rainier Vista Park: Offers sports fields, a splash pad, playgrounds, and a skate park, ideal for families and active visitors.
  • Wonderwood Park: A peaceful neighborhood park with wooded walking trails, tennis courts, and open space.
  • Long Lake Park: A scenic lakeside retreat perfect for swimming, kayaking, and relaxing in the summer months.

🏫 Schools

Lacey is part of the North Thurston Public Schools district, one of the largest in the region:

  • North Thurston High School and Timberline High School: Offering a variety of academic and extracurricular opportunities.
  • Chinook Middle School and Komachin Middle School: Known for preparing students for success in high school.
  • Several elementary schools serve local neighborhoods with strong community involvement and programs.

🎯 Local Attractions

  • Regional Athletic Complex (RAC): A top-tier facility hosting local and regional sports tournaments and community events.
  • Cabela’s Outdoor Store: A large destination store for outdoor enthusiasts featuring gear, displays, and indoor attractions.
  • Lacey Museum: Offers exhibits on the area’s history, growth, and connection to the Puget Sound region.

Lacey blends suburban comfort with easy access to nature, parks, shopping, and schools — making it a top choice for families and commuters alike.
