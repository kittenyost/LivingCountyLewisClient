🌲 Discover Centralia, Washington

🏘️ Listings in Centralia

🏞️ Parks

  • Fort Borst Park: Trails, dog park, lake access, and historic sites.
  • Riverside Park: Skate park and playground near the Chehalis River.

🏫 Schools

Centralia School District serves local students from elementary through high school, offering strong academic and extracurricular programs.

🎯 Local Attractions

  • Centralia Factory Outlets: Popular shopping destination for major brands.
  • Historic Downtown: Filled with charming shops, restaurants, and antique stores in restored historic buildings.

Centralia blends small-town charm with modern convenience, making it a great place to live, work, and explore.
