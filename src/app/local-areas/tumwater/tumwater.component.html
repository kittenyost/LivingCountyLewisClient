🌲 Explore Tumwater, Washington

🏞️ Parks & Nature

  • Tumwater Falls Park: A scenic 15-acre park featuring walking trails, waterfalls, picnic areas, and a salmon viewing station along the Deschutes River.
  • Pioneer Park: A large community park with baseball fields, soccer pitches, play areas, and event spaces.
  • Historical Park: Located near the old Olympia Brewery, it highlights the area's rich past and offers riverfront views.

🏫 Schools

Tumwater School District provides strong academic and extracurricular programs:

  • Peter G. Schmidt Elementary
  • Michael T. Simmons Elementary
  • BLE and Tumwater Middle Schools
  • Tumwater High School and Black Hills High School

🎯 Attractions & Highlights

  • Old Olympia Brewery: While no longer operational, it’s an iconic landmark with historic architecture near the river.
  • Deschutes Valley Trail: A paved trail connecting Tumwater to Olympia and offering beautiful river and forest views.
  • Craft Breweries & Eateries: Tumwater is growing a small but vibrant craft beverage and dining scene.

With a mix of natural beauty, historical roots, and community feel, Tumwater is a great place to live, explore, and connect.
